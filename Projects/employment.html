Apply to join our team!

Server:

Apply to join our staff as a server out the Food Fusion. As a server you will take customers food and drink orders, give the orders to the kitchen, and bring the food to the customers once it is prepared and ready. Those who apply to this position are expected to know the menu and practice strong customer service when dealing with customers.

Hostess:

Join our staff as a hostess. As a hostess you will greet customers with strong customer service and bring them to their table. You will seat them in accordance to the section of tables that is next on the list of sections or in accordance with the size of the party.
